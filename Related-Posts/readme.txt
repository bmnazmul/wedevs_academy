=== Related Posts ===
Contributors: Nazmul Hasan  
Tags: related posts, WordPress, content suggestions, thumbnails, excerpts  
Requires at least: 5.0

This plugin adds a dynamic related posts section to your WordPress site, showcasing post thumbnails, titles, and short excerpts.

== Description ==

The Related Posts plugin automatically displays a list of related posts below your content, helping users discover more relevant content on your site. It dynamically selects posts based on categories and includes thumbnails, titles, and excerpts for better user engagement.

**Features:**
- Automatically shows related posts below your content.
- Displays post thumbnails, titles, and a short excerpt.
- Fetches related posts based on categories.
- Optimized for seamless integration with any theme.

Whether you're running a blog, magazine, or content-heavy website, this plugin keeps your visitors engaged and encourages them to explore more of your content.

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin zip file.
2. Go to **Plugins > Add New** in your WordPress dashboard.
3. Click **Upload Plugin**, then select the downloaded zip file.
4. Click **Install Now** and then **Activate** the plugin.
5. The related posts section will automatically appear below your posts.
